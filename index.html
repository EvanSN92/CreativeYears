Our Programs

We offer age-appropriate classrooms and activities that meet children where they are and help them grow at their own pace.

Infants

6 weeks – 12 months

A calm, nurturing environment where your baby is cuddled, talked to, and engaged with soft sensory activities. We focus on secure attachments, tummy time, and early communication.

Toddlers

1 – 2 years

Busy little explorers need room to move and discover. Our toddler classroom encourages language development, simple routines, and hands-on play with music, art, and movement.

Preschool

3 – 4 years

Preschoolers practice early math and literacy skills through centers, stories, and games. We build independence, problem-solving, and social skills in a fun, structured classroom.

Pre-K

4 – 5 years

Our Pre-K program prepares children for kindergarten with letter recognition, early reading and writing, number sense, science exploration, and social-emotional learning.

After-School

School-age

For school-age children, we offer a safe, supportive space after school with homework help, enrichment activities, and time to relax and play with friends.

Our Approach to Early Learning

Children learn best when they feel safe, loved, and free to explore. We combine play-based learning with developmentally appropriate activities that support the whole child—socially, emotionally, physically, and academically.

  • Hands-on, play-based learning centers
  • Daily music, movement, and story time
  • Early literacy and math woven into play
  • Social-emotional skills: sharing, kindness, problem-solving
  • Outdoor play and gross motor activities
  • Regular communication with families

A Peek at Our Day

  • Morning: Arrival, breakfast, circle time
  • Midday: Learning centers, outdoor play, lunch
  • Afternoon: Rest time, small groups, art & music
  • Late Afternoon: Snack, story time, pick-up

Parent Info: Health & Safety

Your child’s safety is our top priority. Our center follows strict health, safety, and cleaning procedures every day.

Health & Safety Practices

  • Secure check-in and check-out procedures
  • Locked entry and controlled visitor access
  • Age-appropriate classroom ratios
  • Daily cleaning and sanitizing of toys and surfaces
  • CPR and First Aid trained staff

About Creative Year

Creative Year Early Learning Center is a family-run daycare rooted in love, community, and a passion for helping children grow. Our goal is to feel like a second home—warm, welcoming, and full of opportunities to learn.

We believe every child deserves to be seen, heard, and encouraged to be themselves. Through small group activities, individual attention, and lots of hands-on learning, we help children build confidence and a genuine love for learning.

Meet the Director

Deborah Hughes has been caring for children for over 15 years. Her vision for Creative Year is simple: provide a safe, loving space where children are treated like family and guided to grow in their own unique way.

Families choose Creative Year because of the strong relationships, consistent routines, and a warm environment where children feel known and cherished.
